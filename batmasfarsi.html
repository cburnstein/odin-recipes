Batmas Farsi

Recipe Index

Ingredients

  1. 500 grams Young pumpkin or zucchini thinly sliced
  2. 150 grams Raw soybeans shells removed and washed
  3. 150 grams Tomatoes finely chopped
  4. 100 grams Onion sliced
  5. 20 grams garlic finely chopped or crushedmortar and pestle
  6. 20 grams Ginger finely chopped or crushedmortar and pestle
  7. 2 tbsp Olive oil or cooking oil
  8. 1 tsp Paprika powder
  9. 1/2 tsp Coriander Seeds
  10. 1/2 tsp Fenugreek Seeds
  11. 1/2 tsp Cumin powder
  12. 2 tsp Salt
  13. 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  14. 1 tbsp Cilantro for garnish

Steps:

  1. Add onions, cook until translucent.
  2. Add ginger and garlic, cook until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes, cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add pumpkin, soybeans and the rest of the seasonings except cilantro. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Cover the pressure cooker with lid and cook for around 15 minutes on a medium heat.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves while serving.
