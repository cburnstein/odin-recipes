Indianesque Chicken

Recipe Index

Description: An Indian inspired recipe shared with me by my dear Aunt Dot

Ingredients:
  1. 1 ½ pounds of chicken – Costco chicken tenders are great
  2. ¼ cup olive oil
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 teaspoon sugar
  5. 3 garlic cloves – minced
  6. 2 teaspoons paprika
  7. 1 teaspoon cumin
  8. ½ teaspoon coriander
  9. ¼ teaspoon ginger
  10. ¼ teaspoon turmeric
  11. ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  12. 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps: Mix all the spices in the olive oil. Pour the oil into a gallon freezer bag, thrown in the chicken and shake well. You should let it marinade overnight for best flavor before cooking or freezing. You can either barbecue or fry in a fry pan on the stove.
